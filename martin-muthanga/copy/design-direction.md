# Design Direction - Martin Muthanga Portfolio

## Brand Direction
A high-trust, engineering-led portfolio aesthetic that signals precision, delivery discipline, and calm confidence.

## Personality
- Credible
- Grounded
- Strategic
- Reliable
- Results-focused

## Visual Theme
- Modern professional style with structured sections and strong hierarchy
- Technical cues (grid lines, rule dividers, metric panels) used subtly
- Clear contrast and minimal visual noise

## Palette
- Primary: Deep Navy `#0B1F3A`
- Secondary: Steel Blue `#2E4C73`
- Accent: Signal Teal `#1E9E9A`
- Neutral Text: Slate `#3F4A59`
- Background: Soft Cloud `#F4F7FB`
- Surface: White `#FFFFFF`
- Success/Metric Highlight: Emerald `#1F8A5B`

## Typography Mood
- Headlines: Confident geometric sans
- Body: Humanist sans for readability
- Data labels: Compact uppercase sans with generous letter spacing

Suggested pairs:
- Headline: Sora or Montserrat
- Body: Source Sans 3 or Nunito Sans

## Layout and Components
- Sticky top navigation with clear section/page links
- Hero with headline, intro paragraph, and dual CTA
- Trust strip with certifications and region indicators
- Metric cards for quantified impact
- Experience timeline blocks
- Projects cards with outcome-first framing
- Certifications and skills grid
- Contact block with direct email/phone actions

## Interaction and Motion
- Lightweight reveal transitions (fade + rise)
- Subtle hover elevation for cards and CTAs
- Avoid heavy or distracting animations

## Accessibility and UX Requirements
- WCAG-compliant contrast targets
- Semantic heading order and landmark structure
- Keyboard-accessible navigation and controls
- Body text minimum 16px

## Imagery Guidance
- Telecom field operations
- Infrastructure delivery contexts
- Renewable energy implementation visuals
- East Africa context where appropriate

## Content Integrity Rules for Design Team
- Keep all factual metrics and credential details exact.
- Do not alter certification IDs, dates, or numerical outcomes.
- Do not introduce unverified logos, clients, or claims.
