# Design Direction

## Brand Feel
- Professional, trustworthy, and modern
- Friendly and practical (not corporate-stiff)
- Conversion-focused with clear next steps

## Visual Style Language
- Clean card-based sections
- Soft shadows and generous spacing
- Rounded corners for approachable tone
- Realistic professional imagery or illustrations focused on career growth

## Color Direction
- Primary: Deep blue for trust and credibility
- Accent: Warm orange for CTA emphasis
- Neutral base: White + light gray backgrounds
- Success tone (thank-you page): Calm green highlights

## Typography Mood
- Strong, readable sans-serif
- Clear hierarchy with bold headings
- Body text optimized for scannability and mobile reading

## CTA Behavior
- Primary CTA appears in hero, mid-page, and final section
- Button labels must be action-oriented ("Start My Portfolio", "Submit My Portfolio Request")
- Keep CTA contrast high for visibility

## Homepage Layout Priority
1. Hero (value + price + CTA)
2. Trust strip
3. Problem/solution framing
4. How it works
5. Testimonials
6. Top freelancer categories
7. Pricing
8. Final CTA

## Submission Form UX Direction
- Minimal fields above the fold
- Clear labels and helper text
- Strong validation messaging
- Progress reassurance and fee visibility (Ksh 2,500)

## Thank You Page UX Direction
- Clear confirmation state
- Next-step timeline bullets
- Return action buttons
- WhatsApp reminder for fast follow-up

## Content Integrity Rules for Stitch
- Use exact copy from `copy/website-copy.md`
- No placeholder text
- Preserve currency format exactly: `Ksh 2,500`
- Preserve CTA destinations and user flow:
  - Home -> Submit -> Thank You
